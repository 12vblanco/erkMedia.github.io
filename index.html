erk Media Ltd is a research and innovation hub.

Our core ideology is to promote interdisciplinary, evidence-based research, drawing on expertise in emerging technology, ethics, policy, network theory, and the creative arts.

Our experts are selected due to their explicit desire to create positive change for society, regardless of the domain.

We curate technology driven research insights and design content, driven by a commitment to honesty, transparency, and accountability.
